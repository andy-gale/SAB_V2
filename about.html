About Us

Founded in 2025 by college students at UCSB, Share a Bite is a community based platform that seeks to reduce food waste in local communities.

Food waste is a serious problem from individuals throwing away leftovers to commercial food waste. Share a Bite aims to reduce food waste by connecting individuals and businesses with surplus food to those in need.

  • Mission: To reduce food waste in local communities.
  • Vision: To create a world where no one goes hungry.
  • Values: Community, Sustainability, and Compassion.
